Trishul Utsav

Each year on 29th January, we celebrate Trishul Utsav, which is the biggest festival in our school. Trishul Utsav is celebrated in the month of Poush (between mid-December and mid-January). The Trishul Utsav which marks the birth anniversary of Swami Pranavananda

Sports Day


Annual school sports are the most exciting activity in every school. The event is held once a year, usually around December. It is ideal to have school sports in the afternoons during the winter. On sports days, students compete in a variety of events such as 50-100m races, sack races, etc.
